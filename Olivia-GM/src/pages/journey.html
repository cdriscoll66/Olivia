Smart Nav

“Hello, Olivia. Are we going to the dentist?” Olivia confirms. “The ETA is 10am, we’ll arrive 15 minutes early.” The vehicle displays directions onto the augmented reality display to assist Olivia in getting there.

1-3 Year

Augmented reality heads-up display provides turn-by-turn directions.

3-6 Year

As vehicle-to-vehicle and vehicle-to-infrastructure communication takes hold, the system delivers real-time navigation guidance and alerts.

New Horizons

Feeling hungry, Olivia asks her vehicle to find a Mexican restaurant on the route. The vehicle suggests El Toro, best rated place in the area, and displays pictures and reviews on her windshield screen.

1-3 Year

The navigation system integrates the user’s social graph and uses smart analytics to make proactive suggestions based on preferences and interests.

3-6 year

AR HUD projection provides contextual information on passing landmarks. Voice-controlled assistant can contact the restaurant, place a take-away order, or even make a reservation.

Easy Park

As Olivia heads to the opera, the system, the system automatically locates the parking spot nearest to the final destination. Olivia’s vehicle then informs her that it has identified a parking spot nearby. “Shall I navigate there?” The spot is automatically reserved and paid for.

1-3 Year

Vehicle sensors identify vacant spaces along the side of the road as the car drives by and inform other communicating vehicles of these available parking spots.

3-6 Year

A.I. that generates real-time parking maps comes fully online and parking spots can be filtered by need. When fully autonomous, vehicles will park themselves outside of city centers, making urban spaces more liveable.
