Watchdog

Detecting unusual activity near Olivia’s garage, the vehicle streams live footage of the home security system. Fortunately, it’s only kids playing around. Nothing to worry about!

1-3 Year

Users can monitor their homes directly from their vehicle infotainment screen. The system detects home incidents, such as gas or water leak, and informs the users.

3-6 Year

AI-powered cameras will be able to distinguish dogs or friendly passersby from ill-intentioned people. Human-pose detection will allow the system to detect if an older relative falls and immediately inform family members.

Never Apart

It’s bedtime for Jess, Olivia’s youngest daughter, but there’s another hour before getting home. Olivia asks to be connected to Jess’s room.

1-3 Year

In-vehicle communication with users at home is drastically improved with the integration of high-resolution cameras for HD real-time conversations.

3-6 year

With AR-windshield and hologram technology, communication between users at home and in-vehicle enters a new level of fidelity.

Power Up

Once home, Olivia’s vehicle parks itself on the wireless charging station. From her phone, she can access her EV and smart home energy consumption.

1-3 Year

Home and vehicle electricity consumption is accessible via a centralized dashboard that allows users to share power between their house and vehicle and to store or sell excess energy into the electric grid.

3-6 Year

Wireless technology development will make charging EVs even more simple, first on private garage properties and further out, in selected parking spots.
