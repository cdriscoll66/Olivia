Save Time All the Time

Olivia’s got mail. The vehicle informs her that she has received an email from Jason. “Shall I read it to you?” Olivia dictates a response for the system to send back.

1-3 Year

In-vehicle voice recognition can be used to command the system to perform basic functions such as read, dictate, or send emails and meeting requests.

3-6 Year

As this service evolves, vehicle hardware and software will, when desired, turn the vehicle into a mobile meeting room with facing seats, embedded AR screens, 5G internet, and HD cameras.

Accomplish More

Headed to the airport, Olivia is about to lead an important meeting from her vehicle. On her command, the vehicle takes control of the driving and connects her to her meeting.

1-3 Year

The connected vehicle becomes an HD-fidelity video conference hub, allowing users to do meetings on-the-go.

3-6 year

With full autonomy, the system can turn the vehicle into a complete mobile productivity hub with all the same functionality of a fixed work space.

Always Growing

Olivia’s vehicle knows that she is passionate about politics and suggests curated content that helps her stay in the know.

1-3 Year

The service pushes users tailored professional content - articles, podcasts, and tweets - helping them to stay in-the-know and up-to-date with their industry and interests.

3-6 Year
