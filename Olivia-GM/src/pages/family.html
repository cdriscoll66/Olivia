Fleet Calendar

Matthias, Olivia’s 18 year old son, needs the car this Sunday. With just a couple taps on his phone the Chevrolet Malibu is booked for Saturday from 2 to 5pm.

1-3 Year

The vehicles connect to the family’s calendar app, making it as easy as booking a conference room. Users can pre-select vehicle configuration, so when it’s their turn to use the vehicle, the vehicle is set up to their liking.

3-6 Year

Deep analytics and machine learning will be used to draft a weekly and monthly fleet agenda based on the calendars of everyone with access to the vehicle. Users have on-demand access to the family fleet of vehicles.

Peace-of-Mind Parenting

Matthias’ parents are not so worried about him driving the car alone. They know that in case of dangerous driving behaviors, the vehicle’s system will alert them.

1-3 Year

Dangerous driving behaviors, such as speeding or rapid acceleration, are tracked by the system and trigger an alert to parents. Speed limit can be set for young drivers.

3-6 year

In-vehicle biometrics will detect more dangerous driving behaviors, such as alcohol consumption, and the system will automatically react, governing speed or driving itself back to the point of origin if necessary.

Fun for all the family

The whole family is headed to the countryside for a family reunion. Thanks to the immersive games, the long drive to Matthias’ grandparents flies by.

1-3 Year

GM user profiles that are connected to favorite media service providers, such as Spotify, Netflix, Hulu, HBO, Amazon Prime, or Xbox, allow drivers and passengers to access content relevant to their preferences.

3-6 Year

The service integrates blockchain technology to secure in-vehicle content consumption and online shopping.
